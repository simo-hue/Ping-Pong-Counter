Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 19, 2026

We take your digital privacy very seriously. Ping Pong Scoreboard has been engineered from the ground up to respect your personal spaces. We adhere to an absolute data minimalism policy: we do not collect, transmit, share, or monetize any of your personal information.

🔒 Apple App Store Declaration: "Data Not Collected". The app operates 100% locally on your device.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

When you download and use Ping Pong Scoreboard, we do not require, collect, or store any of the following:

  • No registration or accounts (no email, usernames, or passwords required).
  • No contact info or identifiers (no names, phone numbers, or IP tracking).
  • No search history, usage data, or diagnostic data.
  • No device identifier metrics.

2. Zero Third-Party Advertising & Tracking

We do not integrate any third-party advertising SDKs, marketing tags, or analytic tracking suites (such as Google Analytics or Facebook SDK). Your matches, scoring speeds, and usage histories remain entirely private.

3. Device Services & Permissions

Our app requires specific device permissions to deliver premium features, but these inputs never leave your physical device:

  • WatchConnectivity: Used to synchronize active scoring parameters in real-time between your iPhone and your paired Apple Watch.
  • AVAudioSession (Audio & Vocal Referee): Used to announce score increments vocally like a real umpire. Background audio states are managed locally and are never recorded or logged.
  • CoreHaptics: Provides tactile vibrations to confirm tap events on iOS and watchOS devices.

4. Offline-First Infrastructure

All custom configurations, player names, and scoring tables are stored locally inside the application's secure sandbox. The app operates fully without an active internet connection.

5. Contacting Us

If you have questions about our absolute privacy approach, you can contact us via our Support Tab on this website. Any emails you send us for support will only be used to resolve your specific support case and will never be shared with third parties.
