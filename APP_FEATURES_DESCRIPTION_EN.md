## 📝 Full App Store Description

**Argued about who was serving? Forgot if the score was `8-7` or `9-6`? Had the rhythm of a perfect Table Tennis match ruined by simple human error?** 

We’ve all been there. **Ping Pong Counter** is the ultimate, premium companion app for iPhone and Apple Watch designed to solve this exact problem forever. Crafted with high-end, cyberpunk-inspired dark aesthetics, glowing neon scoring fields, and modern Apple APIs, it turns scorekeeping into a fluid, responsive, and gorgeous part of the sport.

Leave your iPhone on a tripod at the net or by the table, and keep score directly from your wrist. With instant synchronization, Live Activities, and a voice synthesizer announcing scores like a real referee, you can focus 100% on your game.

### ✨ Core Features Showcase

#### 📱 1. iPhone Full-Screen Scoreboard (OLED Optimized)
*   **Adaptive Dual Orientation:** Supports both **horizontal** (split screen left/right for tabletop use at the net) and **vertical** layouts (split screen top/bottom for quick one-handed use).
*   **Zero-Interference Gesti-Controls:**
    *   **Tap to Score (`+1`):** Tap anywhere on a player's field to register a point instantly. Features a dynamic floating arcade `+1` animation.
    *   **Swipe Down to Undo (`-1`):** Made a mistake? Swipe down on the player's side to reverse the last action. Holds up to 30 steps of rollback history.
*   **Active Serve Pulse:** A continuous scale-and-glow loop indicator on the serving side, so you always know who has the ball.
*   **Adaptive Visual Net:** A sleek, dashed division line separates the screen to mimic the physical table tennis net.
*   **Instant Player Name Editing:** Click the pencil icon next to player names to edit them inline without leaving the main screen.

#### ⌚ 2. Native Apple Watch Companion App
*   **True Hands-Free Scoring:** Leave your phone at the side of the table and control the match directly from your wrist.
*   **Instant Bidirectional Sync:** Built on high-performance `WatchConnectivity`. Tapping your watch updates your iPhone in real-time, and vice-versa.
*   **Wrist Gestures & Tactile Haptics:** Tap a player column to add a point, long-press to undo. Includes distinct Apple Watch vibration signatures so you can score without even looking at your watch.

#### ⚡ 3. Live Activities & Dynamic Island Integration
*   **Lock Screen Scoreboard:** Lock your phone to save battery. The live score, set count, and active server are projected onto your Lock Screen in real-time.
*   **Dynamic Island Support:** Minimizes into a beautiful, compact status pill while you use other apps. Long-press to open a rich, glassmorphic dashboard overlay.

#### 🔊 4. Premium Vocal Referee & Smart Audio Ducking
*   **Your Personal Umpire:** High-quality English and Italian text-to-speech announces the score and critical highlights (*"Match Point!"*, *"Set Point!"*, *"Deuce!"*).
*   **Smart Audio Ducking:** Automatically lowers background music volume (e.g., Spotify, Apple Music) during announcements and restores it instantly afterward.
*   **Physical Silence Override:** Configured to play even when the phone is on silent mode, so you always hear the score during intense matches.

#### 🛠️ 5. Official ITTF Rules Compliance
*   **Match Customization:** Play standard modern **11-point** games or classic **21-point** formats.
*   **Set Tracking:** Supports Best-of-3 or Best-of-5 sets with glowing visual scoreboard indicators.
*   **Automatic Serve Rotation:** Switches every **2 serves** (for 11-point games) or every **5 serves** (for 21-point games).
*   **Smart Deuce Handling:** Once the score reaches `10-10` (or `20-20`), the serve rotation automatically switches to **1 serve per player** until a player leads by 2 points.
*   **Interactive Manual Override:** Need to adjust the server? Just tap the "SERVE" indicator at any time to reassign rights manually without changing the score.

#### 🎨 6. Exquisite Glowing Cyberpunk Themes
Choose between three professionally curated high-contrast neon palettes:
1.  🔴🔵 **Classic Neon:** Hot Pink & Cyan Blue (Default)
2.  🟢🟣 **Mint & Royal:** Mint Green & Deep Purple
3.  🟠🟢 **Solar Flare:** Solar Orange & Teal Green
*Themes adapt dynamically, pulsing during high-stakes moments like Match Points.*

#### 🔒 7. Absolute Privacy & Offline-First
*   **Zero User Tracking:** No registration, no ads, no trackers, no cookies, and no third-party analytics SDKs.
*   **100% Offline Architecture:** The app requires zero internet access. All data, custom rules, player names, and histories stay entirely on your device.
